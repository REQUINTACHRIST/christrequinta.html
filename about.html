About Us

Released in June 2011, Cars 2 is the 12th animated feature from Pixar Animation Studios and serves as a sequel to the 2006 film Cars. Directed by John Lasseter, the story was conceived while he was traveling worldwide promoting the first film, leading to a shift in focus from small-town racing to international espionage and globetrotting adventures featuring Mater and Lightning McQueen competing in the World Grand Prix. The production was noted for being a technical milestone, as it was the final Pixar film animated using their proprietary Marionette software system before transitioning to Presto in 2012. Despite receiving mixed reviews and being the first Pixar film to hold a "rotten" rating on Rotten Tomatoes, it was a box office success, grossing over $559 million worldwide and becoming the highest-grossing film in the Cars franchise at the time of its release. It introduced new characters like Finn McMissile and Holley Shiftwell, replacing the focus on American racing culture with a spy-thriller format. .
